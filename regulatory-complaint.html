COMPLIANCE AND COMMITMENT.

Acentus is committed to complying with all federal, state and local regulations as well as our accrediting agencies, the Joint Commission and the Healthcare Quality Association on Accreditation. Any customer who feels his/her rights have been denied, who desires further clarifications of rights, or who desires to lodge a complaint or express discontentment with any aspect of service or equipment, should contact Acentus through our main telephone number, without fear of reprisal by the company or its employees. If the issue cannot be resolved by the client services representative, it will automatically be forwarded to the appropriate manager. It is the manager’s responsibility to review all formal complaints and you will be entitled to a written response to your formal complaint.
