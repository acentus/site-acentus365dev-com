Diabetes CGM

Accurate, consistent measurement of blood glucose levels is critical to managing diabetes-related complications. Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) is a method to track glucose levels day and night, then utilizing that data to share with your doctor to optimize your diabetes plan of care. CGM is a small, wafer-like device typically worn discreetly on the arm that continuously tracks your blood glucose levels 24 hours a day. Acentus' New Client Coordinators will work with you and your doctor to navigate the clinical criteria required by Medicare and commercial insurance plan to see if you qualify for a CGM device.
