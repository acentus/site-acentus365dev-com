A message about COVID-19 and your Acentus supply service.

As we all do our part to prepare for and mitigate the impact of COVID-19, I wanted to share with you how Acentus is assuring the continued delivery of vital medical supplies to your home.

Our company is accredited by The Joint Commission, the same organization that accredits 98% of all hospitals in the U.S. As part of this accreditation, Acentus has developed and continually updates a detailed Comprehensive Emergency Plan (CEP) to deal with disasters that would potentially affect any aspect of continuing services to our patients.

Acentus has activated our CEP as a response to the COVID-19 pandemic, mobilizing our service teams to work at home when appropriate. It also increases the interaction with our supply chain partners, leverages alternative product and delivery options and triggers communication tools such as this email for enhanced communication with our patients.

Rest assured, we take the responsibility of supplying your vital medical products to your home very seriously. Our goal on each order is to get those supplies delivered safely, accurately and on time, no matter what event we are facing.
