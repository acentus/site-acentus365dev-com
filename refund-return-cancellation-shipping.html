REFUND, RETURN, CANCELLATION & SHIPPING POLICY

At Acentus, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. We understand that sometimes situations may arise where a refund, return, cancellation, or shipping adjustment is necessary. Please review our policy below:

Refund Policy

  • Refund Eligibility: Refunds are applicable within 30 days from the date of purchase. To be eligible for a refund, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
  • Refund Process: To initiate a refund, please contact our customer service team at service@acentus365.com. Our team will provide you with instructions on how to return the item.
  • Refund Method: Approved refunds will be processed using the original method of payment. Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to be reflected in your account.

Return Policy

  • Return Eligibility: Returns are accepted within 30 days from the date of purchase for defective or damaged items only. Items must be unused and in their original packaging.
  • Return Process: To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at service@acentus365.com. Our team will provide you with instructions on how to return the item.
  • Return Shipping: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a defect or damage caused by the manufacturer.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation Window: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. Once an order has been processed and shipped, it cannot be cancelled.
  • Cancellation Process: To cancel an order, please contact our customer service team at service@acentus365.com with your order details.

Shipping Policy

  • Shipping Methods: We offer standard shipping options. Delivery times may vary depending on warehouse location and the destination.
  • Shipping Restrictions: We currently only ship to addresses within the United States. Additional shipping restrictions may apply to certain products.
  • Shipping Delays: While we make every effort to ensure timely delivery, shipping delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions or carrier issues. We appreciate your patience and understanding in such situations.
